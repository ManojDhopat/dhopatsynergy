About Us

Company Formation Overview (2024)

Established in 2024 under the visionary leadership of Mr. Rohan Panickar, a distinguished personal trainer, mentor, and coach, Amruta is dedicated to revolutionizing health and wellness through value-added products. Our journey began with a commitment to enhancing overall immunity and promoting healthier lifestyles by offering high-quality, natural alternatives to synthetic goods.

Inspired by the “Make in India” initiative, Amruta specializes in sourcing and exporting select local products to global markets. We are passionate about delivering distinctive flavors and health benefits, ensuring that our overseas clients receive not just products, but a touch of authentic, wholesome goodness. Our mission is to provide innovative, health-conscious solutions while showcasing the rich potential of locally sourced ingredients.

Join us as we explore new horizons and offer exceptional, health-boosting products that make a difference in the global marketplace.

Vision And Mission

To connect India’s high-quality, value-added products with the global market, delivering exceptional health and wellness solutions to overseas customers.

To be a leading exporter of locally-sourced products, enriching lives worldwide with authentic, innovative alternatives to synthetic goods.

Function

As a Merchant Exporter (OPC), we specialize in exporting high-value, fruit juices and pulps to the global market, providing select and superior products that meet international standards.
